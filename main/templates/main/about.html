About Us!

5+ Years of Expertise in Solar and Security

At Vimal Solar, we are dedicated to delivering sustainable energy solutions while ensuring cutting-edge security for homes and businesses. With a strong focus on Solar Energy Systems and advanced CCTV Networking, we empower our clients to confidently embrace a greener, safer, and more efficient future.

At Vimal Solar, our commitment goes beyond technology; we believe in building trust and long-term relationships with our clients. Our team combines years of experience with a passion for innovation, ensuring we deliver solutions that meet both present and future needs. Join us in shaping a world that’s not only secure but also powered sustainably for generations to come.

Our Vision

To lead the transition toward a greener and safer world by offering reliable, high-quality, and innovative solutions in solar energy and security networking.

Our Mission

  • To provide cutting-edge solar energy solutions that reduce energy costs and environmental impact.

  • To deliver seamless and secure CCTV networking systems tailored to individual and organizational needs.

  • To build long-term relationships with our clients by ensuring exceptional service and trustworthiness.

Our Journey

Vimal Solar was officially established in 2024 as a GST-registered company and proudly partners with renowned brands like UTL Solar and Adani Solar. However, our roots trace back to 2020, where we honed our skills and built extensive expertise while working under other companies.

Today, with over five years of experience, we excel in delivering high-quality solar power solutions and advanced CCTV systems. From layout design and system planning to installation and ongoing maintenance, we manage every step with precision and dedication.
